We currently opperate our system using a series of ROS bags contained in [Foxglove studio](https://foxglove.dev/). Lots of information on this system can be found on our [github repo](https://github.com/WHOIGit/PhytO-ARM/tree/main). The following pages provide a guide on deployment and opperations of this system. This system utilizes the internal computer of the IFCB as the "brains" of the system. Once the PhytO-Arm software is installed onto your IFCB a series of easy commands allows you to control a variety of sensors and winch positioning.
